Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Claude COD collects, uses, and handles information. This App is a private internal tool used solely for business analytics purposes.

1. Information We Collect

This application accesses data from the Meta Marketing API solely for internal business analytics. The data accessed includes advertising metrics such as spend, impressions, clicks, and conversions associated with our own ad accounts. We do not collect personal data from any third-party users.

2. How We Use the Information

The data accessed through the Meta API is used exclusively for internal business reporting and analytics, monitoring advertising performance, and comparing advertising metrics with sales data. This data is never sold, shared, or disclosed to any third parties.

3. Data Storage

This application does not store Meta user data on any external server. All data is processed locally and is not retained beyond the active session.

4. Third-Party Services

This App uses the Meta Marketing API. Use of the Meta API is subject to Meta's Terms of Service and Data Policy, available at https://www.facebook.com/privacy/policy/.

5. Data Deletion

Since this application does not store any user data, there is no data to delete. For data deletion on Meta's platform, visit https://www.facebook.com/help/delete_account.
